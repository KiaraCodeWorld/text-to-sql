# AI-Powered Hyper-Personalization for Florida Blue Customer Emails

## Overview
Hyper-personalization uses AI to create individually tailored email experiences based on member data, behavior, preferences, and health journey stage.

## Core AI Implementation Strategy

### 1. Data Collection & Integration Layer
**Sources to integrate:**
- Member demographics (age, location, family status)
- Plan type and coverage details
- Claims history and healthcare utilization
- Digital engagement (portal logins, email opens, clicks)
- Preventive care completion status
- Chronic condition management programs
- Preferred communication channels
- Seasonal health patterns in Florida

### 2. AI Technologies to Deploy

#### A. Predictive Analytics
- **Use:** Predict member needs before they arise
- **Example:** Identify members likely to need flu shots based on age, location, and historical patterns

#### B. Natural Language Generation (NLG)
- **Use:** Dynamically write email content
- **Example:** Generate personalized health tips based on member's specific conditions

#### C. Machine Learning Segmentation
- **Use:** Create micro-segments beyond traditional demographics
- **Example:** "Active retirees in Tampa with diabetes who engage digitally"

#### D. Send Time Optimization
- **Use:** AI determines optimal send time per individual
- **Example:** Analyze when each member typically opens emails
